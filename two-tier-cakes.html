Ganache Drip Cakes

Tall drip cakes continue to popular. Choose from the most popular 6 inch size with serves up to 25 people, or a 8 inch cake which serves 35 people. 
All made with 3  layers of sponge, filled with buttercream, jam or curd depending on the flavour, then finished with a glossy milk, white or dark chocolate ganache drip.

They can be themed and decorated almost any way you like — fresh, artificial or dried flowers, chocolates, sweets, macarons, miniature bottles of fizz or gin, fresh fruit.
