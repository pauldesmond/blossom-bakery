Speciality Cakes

At Blossom Bakery we offer an extensive range of cake designs and flavours — for birthdays, gender reveals, baby showers, anniversaries and weddings. We use what we believe are the best ingredients available to deliver a truly unforgettable cake. We always use free-range eggs.

Our cakes are available in 6, 7, 8 and 10 inch sizes. The design of the cake determines its height and the number of servings. We can create a bespoke design from an image or description and tailor-make it especially for you.

All our cakes can be made gluten-free. Selected cakes can be adapted to dairy-free and vegan diets, and we can combine several allergens. To add a personal touch, our acrylic cake toppers can be personalised with your message and come in a range of colours.
