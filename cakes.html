Speciality Cakes

From birthdays and baby showers to anniversaries and just-because moments, each cake is made to order and designed around your brief.

Cakes are available in 6, 7, 8 and 10 inch sizes. Portions vary depending on the style and height, and bespoke designs can be created from your ideas, inspiration photos or colour palette.

Gluten-free options are available across the range, with selected designs adaptable for dairy-free and vegan diets too. Personalised acrylic toppers can also be added for the finishing touch.
