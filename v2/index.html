02 — Meet the baker

Hi, I'm Helen.
I started Blossom in 2018.

After years as a visual merchandising manager and a stint at a local family-run tea room, I set up Blossom Bakery — inspired by a trip to Japan where I fell in love with the cherry blossoms.

Today I bake from a five-star registered kitchen in Great Baddow. Free-range eggs, local produce where I can, and the kind of attention to detail you'd expect from someone who designed shop windows for a living.
