Children's Cakes

The smile on a child's face when they see their birthday cake is something to remember and treasure for years.

We make a wide range of children's cakes — Paw Patrol, princess castles, football clubs, unicorns, Frozen, mermaids, Spider-Man, Toy Story — the list really is endless.

Matching cupcakes & iced biscuits for party bags can be made to coordinate with your cake.

Celebrating two birthdays with one cake? A half-and-half design works beautifully.
Send us your theme along with how many people you need to feed and the date you need it for, and we'll come back with a quote.
