Meet the baker

I'm Helen, the owner of Blossom Bakery.

Based in Great Baddow, Chelmsford, I make a wide selection of freshly baked cakes, cupcakes, traybakes, biscuits, giant cookies and afternoon teas — all designed to look beautiful and taste even better.

I cater for gluten-free, dairy-free and vegan diets, and can combine allergens to suit. I'm fully trained in allergen handling, and pride myself on the flavour I get from every recipe — whatever the constraints.

— Helen Victors
