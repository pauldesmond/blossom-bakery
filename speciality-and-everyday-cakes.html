The flavour menu

Speciality & Everyday Cakes

Over forty signature flavours, from the timeless Victoria sponge to pistachio rose, salted caramel, Biscoff and rainbow. Each cake is baked to order with free-range eggs and ingredients we shop locally where we can.

Available in three sizes — ten inch (14–16 servings), eight inch (12–14) and six inch (10–12). Most can be adapted gluten-free (GF), vegan (V) or both. Add a personalised acrylic topper to make it yours.
