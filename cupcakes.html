Cupcakes

Order yours

Cupcakes are fantastic for all sorts of occasions and a lovely way to share some happiness around. Ideal as gifts for friends and family, and perfect for children's birthday parties. 
Choose from our wide range of designs and delicious flavours — all our cupcakes can be made gluten-free, diary free on request, and selected designs are available for vegan diets.

All cupcakes come in gift boxes of six, twelve, eighteen or twenty-four. Prices start from £20 for a box of six and rise with the design and detail.
