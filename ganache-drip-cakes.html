Ganache Drip Cakes

Tall drip cakes are one of the most-loved styles for celebration cakes right now. Our six-inch tall cakes are made with three to four layers of sponge, filled with buttercream, jam or curd depending on the flavour, then finished with a glossy chocolate ganache drip.

Cut correctly, a six-inch feeds 25 people. We also offer eight-inch cakes serving around 30. They can be themed and dressed almost any way you like — fresh, artificial or dried flowers, chocolates, sweets, macarons, miniature bottles of fizz or gin, fresh fruit. Add a personalised acrylic topper for £6.
