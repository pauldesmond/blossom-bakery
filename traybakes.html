Catering & sharing

Tray Bakes

More than a cupcake, less than a whole cake. Cut into 12 generous slices or 24 smaller portions, our tray bakes are perfect for children’s parties or as an addition at weddings to stretch the portions across guests. 
Victoria sponge tray bakes can be made with a double layer of sponge filled with jam & buttercream. 

A whole tray, shared.

Millionaire’s shortbread, flapjack, old-school sprinkle cake, rocky road, tiffin, brownies and blondies are just a few of the tray bakes we can make.

All traybakes can be made gluten-free (GF) with selected options dairy-free (DF) and vegan (V). Prices start from £25, with vegan chocolate traybakes costing an additional £5 each.
