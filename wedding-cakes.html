For your day

Weddings

Having recently got married myself in October 2024, I completely understand more than ever how important you want your special day to be.

Not only do you need your wedding cake to look perfect, it needs to taste delicious too! Your experience deciding on the cake design and flavours should be a pleasurable, enjoyable and most importantly a stress-free experience. That’s where I can help you. I am professional, approachable, accommodating and will communicate with you all the way through the process. 
We specialise in sponge cakes with naked, ribbed  or fully-covered buttercream finishes. All prices include delivery and set-up within 20 miles of Chelmsford; venues further afield can be quoted.

Please do take a look at some of my favourite cakes in my gallery. I’ve delivered and set up cakes in some beautiful venues including Gosfield Hall, Roslin Beach Hotel, Maiden's Barn, Hylands House, Braxted Park, Apton Hall, Baddow Park, Crondon Park, Leez Priory, and Channels.

We are also Crondon Park's recommended wedding-cake artist.
