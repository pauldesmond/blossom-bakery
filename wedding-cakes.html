Weddings

Having recently married Myself in October 2024 i completely understand more than ever how important you want your special day to be.

Not only do you need your Wedding cake to look perfect, it needs to taste delicious too!Your experience deciding on the cake design and flavours should be a pleasurable , enjoyable and most importantly a stress free experience. That’s where I can help you. I am professional, approachable, accommodating and will communicate with you all the way through the process.

Please do take a look at some of my favourite cakes in my gallery. I’ve delivered and set up cakes in some beautiful venues including Gosfield Hall, Roslin beach hotel , Maidens barn, Hylands House, Braxted Park, Apton hall, Baddow park, Crondon park, Lezz Priory, and Channels.

Latest trend

Vintage piped buttercream.

The Vintage-style Lambeth cakes are one of the trends we keep returning to. Choose from a single heart cake, tiered heart, circular, square or rectangle. Piped with fancy swirls and edged buttercream for that classic vintage feel — bows, cherries and central wording add the showstopper touches.

How it slices

Cake portions.

The size of the slice and how the cake is cut really depends on how many slices you get out of each cake. All of our individual cakes are made with three layers of sponge. As a guide, here’s an estimate of how many slices you can expect from each tiered cake.

Tiered cake portion guide
Tier configurationStacked sizesApprox. servings
Standard 3-tier9, 7 & 5 inch100
Standard 4-tier9, 7, 5 & 3 inch115
Large 3-tier10, 8 & 6 inch125
Large 4-tier10, 8, 6 & 4 inch135

Servings shown are an estimate based on a standard finger-portion cut. Larger dessert-style portions will give fewer servings — happy to advise based on your guest count.
