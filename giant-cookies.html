Giant Cookies

Soft, chewy and stamped with your personal message — a giant cookie is a really lovely gift for any special occasion. All our giant cookies are 11cm round, made with milk, white or dark chocolate chunks of your choice.

Choose from a wide range of colour themes, buttercream finishes, sprinkles, chocolates and sugar-crafted flowers. Send us your personal message and we'll stamp it in the centre. Each cookie comes boxed and ready to give. Gluten-free and vegan options available.
