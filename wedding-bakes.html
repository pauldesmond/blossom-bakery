Wedding Bakes

If a traditional tiered cake isn’t for you, how about a sweet-treat stand?

Brownies, blondies, millionaire's shortbread, tiffin, flapjacks, iced biscuits, cookies and cupcakes are just a few ideas for something a little different.

Selection of sweet treats

Sweet treat stand

These are a great idea if you’d like to have a selection of sweet treats. You can still have your wedding cake, a 6 inch cake for you to cut. Coordinated with a range of biscuits and small cakes of your choice. All of our cupcakes and iced biscuits can be made to tie in with your colour theme. The items can also be personalised with your wedding date, initials, new surname, just married.

Choose from a variety of different flavours for your main cake and cupcakes.

Tell us how many guests you have and your favourite treats and we’ll pull together a quote tailored especially for your special day.

Our sweet-treat stand is available to hire for a small fee and a refundable deposit.
