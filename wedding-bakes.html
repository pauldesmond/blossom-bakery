For your guests

Wedding Bakes

Not every wedding needs a tiered centrepiece. Helen makes the bakes that fill out a sweet table, sit alongside your cake, or replace it altogether — sweet treats, taster cupcakes for tasting visits, and personalised favours for your guests to take home.

Beyond the tier

Sweet treat stand

If the traditional tiered wedding cake isn’t for you maybe a selection of sweet treats combined with a smaller cake? We make a large selection of brownies, millionaire shortbread, cookies, cupcakes, blondies and iced personalised biscuits. We also hire out our Sweet treat stand for a small fee.

Pricing depends on how many items you require. Tell us how many guests you have and we will pull together a quote based on your favourite items and your numbers

We have many years of experience in making wedding cakes, so that you can trust that your ideas will become reality. No idea is too big or too small.

At Blossom Bakery it is important to us that the service you receive from us is just as special as your wedding day. We will listen to your ideas, sketch a design for you to review and, most importantly, ensure your ideas and vision is successfully delivered. We ensure all of our clients receive a professional, friendly and reliable service when planning their wedding cake.

For your guests

Favours

In addition to making your wedding cake we can also make your wedding favours for you and your wedding guests to enjoy. These include cupcakes, personalised iced biscuits and macarons. These make the perfect addition to your guests place setting on the table. We are able to co-ordinate these with your wedding cake and colour theme.

All of our favours are individually presented in a selection of beautiful packaging for you to choose from. These can also be personalised.
